HELLO

I'm Aimee. I'm a painter, creative artist, entrepreneur, private pilot, risk taker and I'm in the process of getting my Masters in Creative Arts Therapy Counseling (MACAT-2019). Woohoo!

I truly love the creative process and there is nothing better than holding the space for you to create and connect with your inner badass. Why badass? Because as stuff comes up, it needs to come out. And that's what we will be working with, the incredible power of intuition combined with creativity, connection and expression.
