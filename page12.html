Art and Mindfulness for Teens Series, $195

Saturdays: 1-2:30am -- Oct. 12th, 19th, Nov. 2nd & 9th.
Cost includes all four sessions, all materials included, no experience necessary!


Teens and college students are living with record levels of anxiety and stress. Many rely upon unhealthy coping mechanisms which leave a negative social, emotional, physical impacts on self, family and community. Art-making in a therapeutic environment not only reduces stress, depression and anxiety in teens and young adults, but it is rooted in spiritual expression and analysis. When a symbol arises in the therapeutic process, which exhibits a dysfunction, it also provides a valuable path forward in which to heal. The psychotherapeutic benefits of art therapy in conjunction with meaning-making and spirituality affirm “personhood and identity, and an increased sense of personal validation and worth” (Bell, 2011).

Bell, S. (2011). Art therapy and spirituality. Journal for the Study of Spirituality, 1(2), 215-230.

Art and Archetypes for Women Intro Series $195

Saturdays: 10-11:30am -- Oct. 12th, 19th, Nov. 2nd & 9th
Cost includes all four sessions, all materials included, no experience necessary!

We will create art and do exploratory work around where we have been and where we see ourselves in the future, using the structure of archetypes. Are we entering a transitional period? Where do we fit into the puzzle? What are our roles. We will focus on our roles as daughter, sister, mother, companion. Whether or not you have children or siblings, as women we still fill these roles for others in our lives. Together and individually, we will explore (with levity and fun!) the specific meaning we have assigned to each role. We will use art-making to explore the somatic (or of the body) connection to increase mindfulness and awareness around automatic thoughts which then evolve into feelings. Through this process (remember art therapy is all about the process not the product!) we begin to identify our archetypes as a means to better understand our passions and our own personal tapestry.

  • Where? Ridgefield Art Therapy Studio Space
